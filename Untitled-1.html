  1. Football Tournaments:
  • World Cup:
The inaugural edition took place in Uruguay in 1930.
  • UEFA European Championship (Euro):
Started in 1960, held every four years.
  • UEFA Champions League:
Commenced in 1955 as the European Cup, it is the premier club competition in Europe.
  • Copa America:
The oldest continental football competition, inaugurated in 1916.
